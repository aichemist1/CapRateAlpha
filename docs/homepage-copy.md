# CapRateAlpha Homepage Copy

## Recommended Hero

### Headline

Fill your retail vacancies faster. With marketing that looks like it came from a brokerage team.

### Subheadline

For the spaces your broker doesn't prioritize, CapRateAlpha gives you professional leasing collateral, a tenant pipeline, and a workflow to move them yourself.

### ROI Support Line

A single filled vacancy on a 5-year lease typically saves $10,000 to $20,000 in leasing commission. CapRateAlpha pays for itself the first time it works.

### Market-Truth Support Line

The tenants expanding fastest right now, including QSR concepts, boutique fitness, wellness services, and food-and-beverage operators, are actively looking for small-bay space in suburban corridors. CapRateAlpha helps you find them before your broker does.

## Why This Message Works

- speaks directly to the owner who already feels broker neglect on smaller vacancies
- avoids forcing an anti-broker narrative
- anchors value in faster execution and professional presentation
- ties subscription cost to a clear commission-avoidance ROI story
- introduces tenant demand language that feels informed by current market behavior

## Primary CTA

Publish Your Vacancy

## Landing Page Attribution

Public vacancy landing pages should include visible `Powered by CapRateAlpha` attribution by default.

White-label removal should be reserved for the Premium tier.

## Notes For Design

- the hero should show a broker-grade flyer, landing page, and social asset, not abstract SaaS graphics
- the page should visually reinforce that this is for small-bay retail vacancy execution
- the ROI line should appear above the fold
- the primary CTA should lead directly into the Day 1 onboarding flow from the product spec
